Before You Book — Everything You're Probably Wondering

Most full packages take 2–3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We'd rather take the time to do it right than rush through it.

Both work. We offer mobile detailing across Orillia and the surrounding area, or you're welcome to drop off — whichever fits your schedule better.

A proper wax forms a protective seal, so rain won't undo the work — it'll actually bead and run off cleaner than before. Just give it a few hours to cure before the first wash.

A few days' notice is ideal, especially during seasonal rushes (spring cleanup and pre-winter protection are our busiest windows), but we'll always try to fit you in sooner if we can.
